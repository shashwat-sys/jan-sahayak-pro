# From Complaint to Remedy

## Final Participant Agenda

**Audience:** PLVs, frontline workers, young activists, and community leaders  
**Resource persons:** Nawaz, Nagmani, Tausif  
**Supervision:** Sr Anupriya  
**Duration:** 1 day, `9:30 AM-6:00 PM`  
**Format:** interactive, practice-based, participant-centered

## Purpose of the training

This training is designed to help participants respond more confidently and strategically to common community justice problems. The focus is on practical legal action, not abstract legal theory.

By the end of the day, participants should be better able to:

- identify when a community problem becomes a legal issue
- write stronger complaints, FIR-style applications, and representations
- deal with police in a calm, safe, and strategic way
- link cases to social security and welfare pathways
- document authority inaction and plan the next escalation step
- understand when a matter should be referred for legal aid or High Court action

## Core message for participants

**First refusal is not the end of the road.**

Field method for the day:

**Listen -> Record -> Classify -> Draft -> Submit -> Follow up -> Escalate -> Refer**
